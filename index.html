At Aquarius Medical Clinic, we are pleased to offer same day appointments or walk-in medical care. You do not need to be a registered clinic patient in order to attend our clinic. We are a full service medical clinic. Once your medical issue has been assessed we will be able to assist you to find the most appropriate treatment.

Examples of care services offered at our clinic include:
  • Prescription renewal
  • Ordering medical imaging
  • Arranging for laboratory testing
  • Providing preventive care
  • gynecological examination
  • Counselling on contraceptive options
  • Referral to a medical or surgical specialist
We provide follow-up care, including reviewing test results and providing solutions for your medical issues. Our doctors are all licenced with the BC College of Physicians and Surgeons and they provide highly skilled and compassionate medical care.
